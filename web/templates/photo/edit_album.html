Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the order that my Photo Albums will be listed?

Your list of photos albums are sorted as follows:

  • Pinned albums are first, and always stay in the front.
  • Then they are sorted by most recently 'updated.'

To 'bump' an album back to the front of your list, open its Edit Settings page (this page) and click on 'Save Changes' to bump its 'last updated' time.

How do I change the Cover Photo for my album?

To change the Cover Photo for your album, follow these steps:

  1. Visit your photo album.
  2. Check the box for only one photo in the album.
  3. At the bottom of the page, click on the "Update Album Entry" button.
