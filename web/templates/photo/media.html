My Media

About This Page

This page shows you all of the media (images) that you have uploaded to {{PrettyTitle}}, including your Gallery photos and any content that you have shared on the forums.

All members are given a limited quota of disk space allowed for their media on this website. You can see your current used storage on this page. If you have run out of storage space, you can use this page to delete older files to free up room for new ones.

How many photos can I upload?

Currently, you are allowed to store {{FormatFilesize .Quota.MaxSize}} worth of media.

To set some context, 100 photos on your Gallery will typically use up about 11 MiB of storage. So, you should be able to share at least 200 photos across your Gallery and forum posts before you run low on space. The exact count may vary depending on the size of each photo.

Note: The right-click blocker is disabled on this page, so you may download your own pictures from this page by right-clicking on them (or long pressing on mobile).
