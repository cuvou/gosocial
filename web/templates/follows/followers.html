Followers vs. Friends

On this page, you can manage your Followers list.

Followers may receive notifications when you post new content on the website (such as photos, videos or blog posts) which they are allowed to see.

By default, you will automatically Follow all of your Friends. But you may also Follow people who are not your friends, as well as Unfollow certain friends if you don't care to hear about the new content they have posted.

Note: "Friends-only content" that you post will only notify your followers who are also on your Friends list; and "Private" content will only notify followers who you have shared your private content with.
