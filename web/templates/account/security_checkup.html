Two Factor Authentication can help to protect your account even if somebody manages to figure out your password. In 2025, it is increasingly common that compromised web browser add-ons are stealing peoples' passwords, even if you make use of a password manager.

By enabling Two Factor Authentication, you can use an app on your phone that provides a temporary six-digit code to log in to your account, in addition to your password. This prevents hackers who steal your password from getting into your account, as they do not have physical access to your phone to get these temporary rotating codes.
