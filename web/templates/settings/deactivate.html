Deactivate My Account

If you'd like to take a break from {{PrettyTitle}} but think you may want to come back later, you may temporarily deactivate your account.

While deactivated, your {{PrettyTitle}} account will be hidden from everybody and will appear the same as if you had fully deleted it. However, you will be able to log back in later and restore your account, and pick up where you left off, and it will be like you never left!

If you later decide that you want to permanently delete your account after all, you can also log in later and do so without needing to re-activate it first.

This option is recommended because it is less destructive than a full account deletion: because if you then changed your mind, you would need to start all over again with a new account, wait for your new certification photo to be approved, and then try and reconnect with your old friends again.
