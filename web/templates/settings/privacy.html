This page is your "one stop shop" to control your overall privacy and interaction preferences on {{PrettyTitle}}.

With the settings available on this page, you may control:

  • Who can see your profile page on the website at all.
  • Who you want to interact with (control who can slide into your DMs, comment on your photos, see your friends list, or share their private pics with you).
  • Who can search for you on the Member Directory (e.g. whether you want to be hidden from search results by age, gender, or orientation).

The default is that users must be logged-in to even look at your profile page. If your profile URL (/u/{{.CurrentUser.Username}}) is visited by a logged-out browser, they are prompted to log in.

Your profile is fully visible to logged-in users, but if a logged-out browser visits your page they will see a very minimal view: only your profile picture and display name are shown. Preview

If you check this box then only friends who you have approved are able to see your profile page and gallery. Your gallery photos also will NOT appear on the Site Gallery page. If your profile page is visited by a logged-out viewer, they are prompted to log in.

Note: this refers to Direct Messages on the main website (not inside the chat room).

Almost any member of the site may send you a Direct Message from your profile page (except for maybe Shy Accounts).

Nobody can slide into your DMs except for friends (and admins if needed). Anybody may reply to messages that you send to them.

Nobody can start a Direct Message conversation with you on the main website (except an admin if necessary). Anybody may reply to messages that you sent to them first.

When somebody Follows you, they will be notified when you upload a new photo, video or blog post which they have permission to see.

Any member of the website is allowed to follow you.

Only your friends may follow your profile. Upon accepting their friend request, they will follow you automatically.

Any certified member who can see your profile page can see your friend list.

Only your friends can see who else is on your friends list.

Nobody but yourself (and website admins) are able to see your friends list.

Any member of the site who can see your photo can leave a comment on it.

Only your approved friends are allowed to post comments on your photos.

Nobody can add comments to your photos. Note: existing comments will still remain.

This setting can help you to be in control of who else on {{PrettyTitle}} is allowed to unlock their private photo gallery for you.

Any member of the website is able to share their private photo gallery with you.

Only people who you have accepted as a friend will have the ability to share their private photo gallery with you.

People on your friend list and people who you have sent a Direct Message to (on the main website - not the chat room) will be able to share their private photos with you. Note: for example, if somebody sends you an unsolicited DM and you did not respond, that person can not share their private photos with you.

Nobody on the website will be allowed to share their private gallery with you. Note: this will mean that you will have no method to see private photos on the site except those which had already been shared with you in the past.

These settings allow you to "opt-out" and be hidden from search results for certain fields such as your gender or sexual orientation.

Any search that includes age range filters will hide you from the results.

Any search that includes a gender filter will hide you from the results.

Any search that includes an orientation filter will hide you from the results.
